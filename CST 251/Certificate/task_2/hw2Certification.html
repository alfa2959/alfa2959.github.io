Björk

The Icelandic singer

Photograph of singer Böjrk performing at Olavsfest 2022. Credit: Santiago Felipe/Redferns for ABA

Here's a time line of Björk's life:

  • 1965 - Born in Reykjavík, Iceland
  • 1993 - Released her debut album "Debut"
  • 1994 - Earned her first Grammy nomination for "Human Behaviour"
  • 2002 - Became the first solo female act to headline Coachella
  • 2004 - Performed at the 2004 Summer Olympics
  • 2007 - Headlined Coachella for the second time
  • 2020 - Joined the film cast for "The Northman"
  • Present - Will release her 10th album, "fossora," on September 30, 2022

"If you want to make something happen that hasn't happened before, you've got to allow yourself to make a lot of mistakes" - Björk
